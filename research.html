🔬 Research Areas:-

🌊 Flood Risk & Hazard Assessment – Flood mapping, vulnerability analysis, flood modeling using GIS, Remote Sensing, SAR and Landsat data.

🌡️ Urban Thermal Studies – Urban Heat Island (UHI) and Urban Cold Island (UCI) analysis using Landsat derived LST, spatial mapping, and trend assessment.

🌍 Climate Change & Variability – Climate data analysis, trend detection, impact assessment using historical and projected datasets (IMD, CMIP6), including SAR-derived environmental monitoring.

⛰️ Land Subsidence & Geohazards – Monitoring land subsidence, urban settlement stability, and integrated hazard mapping using SAR Interferometry (InSAR) and geospatial techniques.

⚠️ Disaster Risk Management – Hazard assessment, vulnerability mapping, and mitigation strategies for climate-induced disasters.

👥 Socio-Economic & Social Inequality Studies – Analysis of social vulnerability, inequality mapping, and integration of demographic and environmental data.

📊 Geospatial & Spatial Statistics Applications – Frequency Ratio, Weight of Evidence, Certainty Factor, Logistic Regression, and multi-criteria decision-making for hazard and vulnerability modeling.

🤖 Applied AI & Machine Learning in Environmental Studies – Predictive modeling for flood, groundwater, hazard, and thermal mapping using ensemble algorithms (Random Forest, SVM, Boosting, etc.).

🛠️Research & Technical skills:-

🛰️ Remote Sensing & GIS – Satellite image processing, LULC classification, DEM analysis, flood & groundwater mapping; experienced with SAR, Landsat, Sentinel, and Planet Scope datasets.

💻 Geospatial Software – QGIS, ArcGIS, ERDAS Imagine, ENVI, SNAP, Conefor, Guddos, Google Earth Engine.

🤖 Applied AI & Machine Learning – Supervised & unsupervised learning, ensemble methods (Random Forest, SVM, AdaBoost, Gradient Boosting, XGBoost, LightGBM, CatBoost), predictive environmental & hazard modeling.

📊 Statistical Analysis & Programming – SPSS, R, Python (pandas, scikit-learn, matplotlib); raster/vector data handling and automation of geospatial workflows.

📈 Spatial Statistics – Logistic Regression, Frequency Ratio, Weight of Evidence (WOE), Certainty Factor.

🌦️ Climate & Hydrological Data Analysis – IMD datasets, CMIP6 climate projections, Mann-Kendall, Sen’s Slope.

🗺️ Digital Cartography & Visualization – Thematic map design, uncertainty mapping, geospatial data visualization.

🧭 Surveying & Instruments – Prismatic Compass, Theodolite, Total Station, Dumpy Level, GPS-based mapping.

🚶Field Survey – Socio-economic surveys, ground-truthing, primary data collection.
